PRIVACY POLICY FOR WHALEY CHROME EXTENSION

Last updated: [Current Date]

This Privacy Policy describes how the Whaley Chrome extension ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our language learning extension.

1. DATA WE COLLECT

1.1 Browsing Data

  • Page content and text from websites you visit
  • URLs of pages you browse (to apply translations)
  • Basic browsing activity to detect page navigation

1.2 Language Learning Data

  • Your selected target language preference
  • Words you interact with on web pages
  • Vocabulary progress and mastered words
  • Translation history and learning statistics

1.3 Audio Data

  • Voice recordings when using the audio chat feature
  • Speech-to-text conversions of your voice input

1.4 User Preferences

  • Extension settings and configurations
  • Language selection preferences
  • UI customization settings

1.5 Authentication Data

  • User account information (if you choose to log in)
  • Email address and basic profile information

2. HOW WE USE YOUR DATA

2.1 Language Learning Features

  • Translate web page content to help you learn new languages
  • Track your vocabulary progress and mastered words
  • Provide personalized learning recommendations
  • Store your learning history for progress tracking

2.2 Audio Processing

  • Convert your voice recordings to text for language practice
  • Process audio input for pronunciation assistance
  • Enable voice-based language learning interactions

2.3 Personalization

  • Remember your language preferences
  • Customize the extension interface based on your settings
  • Provide tailored vocabulary suggestions

2.4 Extension Functionality

  • Apply translations to web pages you visit
  • Save your preferences and settings
  • Enable core extension features

3. WHO WE SHARE DATA WITH

3.1 Third-Party Services

  • Google Cloud Translation API: For text translation services
  • OpenAI API: For speech-to-text conversion and AI-powered language assistance
  • Google Cloud Vision API: For image translation features (if applicable)

3.2 Data Sharing Limitations

  • We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data
  • We do not share your data with advertisers
  • We do not use your data for marketing purposes
  • Third-party APIs only receive the minimum data necessary for their services

3.3 Data Processing

  • All data processing is done securely through established APIs
  • We do not store your raw audio recordings
  • Translation requests are processed in real-time and not permanently stored

4. DATA STORAGE AND SECURITY

4.1 Local Storage

  • Vocabulary progress and settings are stored locally in your browser
  • User preferences are saved in Chrome's extension storage
  • No sensitive data is stored on our servers

4.2 Cloud Storage (Optional)

  • If you choose to log in, your learning progress may be synced to cloud storage
  • Cloud data is encrypted and protected
  • You can opt out of cloud storage at any time

4.3 Security Measures

  • All API communications use HTTPS encryption
  • We follow industry-standard security practices
  • Your data is protected against unauthorized access

5. USER RIGHTS AND DATA CONTROL

5.1 Access and Control

  • You can view and manage your stored data through the extension settings
  • You can delete your vocabulary history and learning progress
  • You can reset all extension data to factory defaults

5.2 Extension Removal

  • You can disable or remove the extension at any time
  • Removing the extension will delete all locally stored data
  • Cloud-stored data (if you logged in) will be retained unless you specifically request deletion

5.3 Data Deletion

  • Local data is automatically deleted when you remove the extension
  • Cloud data can be deleted by contacting us
  • We will respond to data deletion requests within 30 days

5.4 Opt-Out Options

  • You can disable specific features (audio recording, translation, etc.)
  • You can choose not to log in and use only local storage
  • You can disable data collection for specific features

6. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

  • Our extension is not intended for children under 13
  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
  • If you are a parent and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us

7. CHANGES TO THIS PRIVACY POLICY

  • We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time
  • We will notify users of significant changes through the extension
  • Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy

8. CONTACT INFORMATION

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at:

  • Email: christinemtr1@gmail.com
  • Extension Support: Through the Chrome Web Store

9. DATA RETENTION

  • Local data: Retained until you remove the extension or clear browser data
  • Cloud data: Retained until you request deletion or delete your account
  • API processing data: Not permanently stored, processed in real-time only

10. COMPLIANCE

  • We comply with applicable data protection laws
  • We follow Chrome Web Store privacy requirements
  • We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data

This Privacy Policy is effective as of the date listed above and applies to all users of the Whaley Chrome extension.
